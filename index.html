I was born in Tours, but I spent my childhood in many cities of France, I even lived and studied abroad. Consequently, I got to experience schooling in many institutions.
Studying in multiple schools throughout my education offered me several benefits like academic flexibility, increased independence, and even cultural awareness.
Ever since I was a child, I always loved reading and expressing my thoughts through paintings. Sports also helps me clear my mind, it has mental and physical impacts on me: it helps a lot with focusing and discipline. I also love helping people in need, that is why I have always participated in outreach activities.
Currently, I am a student at the University of Blois, where I study networks and telecommunications and enjoy my classes!
During my final year of high school, I got to work on many engineering projects like an Ashtray Robot. Thanks to these projects, I acquired many hard skills like the integration of information technologies into technical projects the ability to design technical solutions meeting specific needs, and some soft skills like adaptability and teamwork.
