What Are My

Interests

I love to read during my free time, whether it's in public transports or in bed. Reading allows me to immerse myself into another world and forget about the one around me, whether it's fiction expanding my imagination or non-fiction satisfying my desire for knowledge.
The same way, sports embody a fusion of the body and the mind. It's more than just physical activity, it's a constant quest for personal improvement. Through sports, I explore my limits, develop discipline, and cultivate resilience.
Through the cultures we encounter and the landscapes we discover, travel creates unforgettable memories. It also expands our perspectives, immersing us in the diversity of the world.
I also try to learn some basics of new languages during my free time. Each language opens a door to a deeper understanding of customs, traditions, and ways of thinking. It's also a stimulating exercise for the brain and strengthens memory.
